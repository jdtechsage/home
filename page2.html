04 / FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take? +
Timelines vary by scope. A landing page takes 2–3 weeks, a full-stack app 2–4 months, and a game 3–8 months. We provide detailed project timelines during scoping.
Do you work with early-stage startups? +
Absolutely. Some of our best work has been with pre-seed startups. We offer flexible payment structures and can work with equity as part of compensation in some cases.
What's your revision policy? +
The Scale and Sage tiers include unlimited revisions during the active project. Revisions outside scope or post-launch are billed at our hourly rate of $120/hr.
Can we hire you for ongoing retainer work? +
Yes, and we love it. Retainer arrangements starting at $6,000/month give you dedicated hours, priority support, and a long-term strategic partner rather than a one-off vendor.
Who owns the code and IP? +
You do. 100%. Upon final payment, all code, assets, and intellectual property transfer to you. We use clean, well-documented code so you can hand it off to any team.
Do you sign NDAs? +
Always. We take confidentiality seriously and are happy to sign NDAs before any conversation begins. Protecting your idea is as important to us as building it.
